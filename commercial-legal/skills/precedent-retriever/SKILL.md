# precedent-retriever

## Purpose
Searches closed matters for similar cases to inform the current review.

## Inputs
- Counterparty name, matter type, key clauses

## Steps
1. Search Airtable closed matters for similar counterparty or matter type
2. Surface the top 2-3 most relevant precedents
3. Note what was accepted or rejected in those matters

## Outputs
- List of relevant precedents with outcome summary

## Notes for Lawyers
Precedents are suggestions only. The assigned lawyer makes the final call.
